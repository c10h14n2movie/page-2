As the name suggests, Hashtag for Jekyll is an elegant and contemporary theme for Jekyll, a powerful static site generator.

Key Features

Don’t let yourself be fooled by the minimal and tidy overall appearance of this theme — you might be surprised what’s included.

For a complete list of features including examples and descriptions, please visit the theme preview.

  • Minimal & Responsive Design Focused on Typography
  • Subtile Animations
  • Retina Ready — only SVG’s
  • Decent Parallax Scrolling
  • Estimated Reading Duration Indicator
  • Clean, Valid HTML5 & CSS3
  • Author Bio & Social Sharing
  • Media Shortcodes for Images, Videos on YouTube / Vimeo & Music on Soundcloud
  • Disqus Comments
  • Syntax Highlighting
  • Dynamic RSS Feed
  • Google Analytics & Web Fonts Support
  • Advanced Pagination
  • Next Article Teaser
  • Twitter Cards & Facebook Open Graph Support
  • Multiple Page Types
  • Sketch 3 Layered Image Source Included

Hope you enjoy it!
